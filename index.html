WHAT DO WE OFFER?

Smart Pickup

This app is to ensure that the kids are handed to authorized personnel only at the drop point. Further the app makes it easy to substitute in case the authorised person is not available. A photo of the substitute needs to be uploaded through mobile ,which will automatically reflect on the conductor app thus making it safe for the kid. All this, at a click of a button, on their smart phones once again making the entire process hassle free and exact for parents.

Smart Attendance

This mobile app automates attendance for teachers by recording it when students enter campus. Teachers can verify and edit records. If a student forgets their ID or is absent despite recorded attendance, teachers can manually adjust. Edited attendance is stored digitally in the database for future use by the school.

In School Zoning

This is a solution to track students movements inside the school and tab trespassing , if any, into vulnerable or prohibited areas. Appropriate alerts to the admin are triggered for necessary administrative action. This feature is of importance, especially in vast campuses and boon to schools, having large number of students.

Route Optimization

This is an application, which helps the school transport department, to efficiently create bus routes depending on the pickup / drop points, bus capacity and the distances to be travelled. This process eliminates the random allocation of students to buses thus optimising the bus utilization resulting in cost efficiency without compromising on the quality. The Students are optimally distributed without overcrowding buses or underutilizing the capacities in some routes.

Parents & Visitors Management

Secuconnect School provides a smart visitor management system for schools. The Secuconnect School mobile app offers instant registry and photo verification for parents and visitors, eliminating manual entry. Security at the gate authenticates each visitor with photos before granting access. Digital records with photos are stored for easy access, and multiple customizable reports can be generated.

Services

This app is uses centralized command centre which monitors all the buses, campus readers ,class attendance, in campus tracking ,video tracking of students. Timely reports are provided to the school management for their use and appropriate actions. A dedicated team of trained field engineers and relationship managers handle the queries and provide technical support. Each school is provided with a dedicated Secuconnect manager to ensure proficiency .
